HONOR CODE

Last updated May 24, 2026

Assignment Help Global is committed to operating ethically and responsibly. This Honor Code sets out the principles that guide our platform and the obligations we expect from every user. By using our services, you agree to uphold this code.

1. Our Purpose

Assignment Help Global exists to connect students and learners with qualified academic experts who can help them understand complex topics, improve their writing, and develop their skills. Our service is a legitimate educational assistance platform — similar to a tutoring or coaching service — that provides model work, explanations, and guidance to support your learning journey.

We do not encourage or support academic dishonesty. The materials we provide are intended to be used as study aids, reference examples, and learning resources — not to be submitted as original work in violation of your institution's policies.

2. Acceptable Use by Students

You may use our services to:

  • Receive expert explanations of difficult concepts or topics
  • Review a model essay or paper to understand structure, tone, and formatting
  • Get feedback, editing, or proofreading on your own original work
  • Use sample work as a guide to improve your own writing and research skills
  • Prepare better for exams or class discussions by studying an expert-written summary
  • Complete non-graded personal or professional projects

3. Your Responsibility Regarding Academic Integrity

Every educational institution has its own academic integrity policy. It is your sole responsibility to:

  • Understand and comply with your institution's policies on the use of external academic assistance
  • Ensure that any work you submit for academic credit is your own original work, or properly attributed if permitted
  • Use materials provided by our experts solely for learning, reference, and personal study

Assignment Help Global is not liable for any academic consequences resulting from a student's misuse of our services.

4. Our Experts' Commitment

All experts on our platform are expected to:

  • Deliver original, plagiarism-free work for every assignment
  • Respect the confidentiality of each client's information and project details
  • Maintain professional and respectful communication at all times
  • Only accept projects within their verified areas of expertise
  • Disclose any potential conflict of interest before beginning a project

Experts who violate our standards are removed from the platform immediately.

5. Originality and Plagiarism

All work delivered by Assignment Help Global experts is written from scratch and is original. We do not resell or reuse previously delivered work. Our experts are required to produce unique content for every project. If you believe work delivered to you is plagiarized, contact us immediately at support@assignmenthelpglobal.com and we will investigate and resolve the issue promptly.

6. Prohibited Conduct for Users

You must not use our platform to:

  • Request work intended to facilitate cheating in a graded, invigilated, or time-limited examination
  • Resell or commercially distribute work provided by our experts
  • Request content that is illegal, defamatory, discriminatory, or harmful
  • Impersonate another person when submitting a project request
  • Attempt to circumvent our security measures or abuse our payment system

We reserve the right to refuse service or terminate accounts for any violations of this Honor Code.

7. Consequences of Violations

Any user found to be violating this Honor Code may face:

  • Immediate suspension or permanent termination of their account
  • Forfeiture of any pending refund or credit
  • Reporting to relevant authorities where required by law

8. Reporting a Concern

If you become aware of any misuse of our platform — by a user or an expert — we encourage you to report it. Your report will be treated confidentially and investigated promptly. Contact us at support@assignmenthelpglobal.com.

9. Updates to This Code

We may update this Honor Code from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the current Honor Code.
